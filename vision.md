The vision of Sentinel is simple.

Humans have to keep with a torrential flood of information. These include
- news
- emails
- messages
- social media feeds (instagram, X, etc.)

In essence, these are streams of data. Being able to respond and take action on new information is very important.
You want to move fast. (https://blog.samaltman.com/super-successful-companies)

Sentinel aims to help. Sentinel is not about being lazy - about ignoring your emails and having an LLM help you scrape by.

No, with Sentinel, you should be able to process and keep up with a monumental amount of information and find signal

Sentinel should also promote peace-of-mind.
